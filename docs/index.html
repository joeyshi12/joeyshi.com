About

Hello! I’m a software developer and BSc student majoring in Computer Science and Math at the University of British Columbia.

As a developer of many projects, I tackle challenging problems head-on. My experiences with using unfamiliar tools have made me a versatile problem-solver. I consider myself to be a reliable and efficient team member.
